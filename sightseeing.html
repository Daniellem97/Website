Sightseeing Tours

Here at Gillian's we offer many opportunities to see Galway in all its glory. Facilities nearby include - Eyre Square Shopping Centre, two sandy beaches, spectacular coastal walks, a variety of restaurants within walking distance, friendly traditional Irish music pubs, horse-riding, golfing, fishing, and a variety of water sports. Bikes are also available for hire

Sightseeing tours can be arranged at the B&B at a discount for: Connemara, Cliffs of Moher, The Aran Islands and a bus tour of Galway City. All tickets can be purchased on arrival.

However, if you would like to book you sightseeing in advance, we have some options available.
